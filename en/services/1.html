Warm kneading - reduces heart rate by 10-15 beats per minute

These are not just techniques – they are scientifically proven ways to activate your body's natural healing processes. Massage triggers the parasympathetic nervous system, responsible for recovery and regeneration.

Questions the Heart Asks

Do I need classical massage?
If you're asking this question, you probably do. But actually, this isn't a question of need – it's a question of love. Every person deserves a moment when someone cares for them. Classical massage is that moment.
Can it be painful?
Classical massage is like a warm embrace – it should never hurt. Our therapists listen to your body and your words. If something doesn't feel right, we adjust immediately. Your comfort is our priority.
How might I feel after the massage?
Imagine how you feel after the best sleep of your life. Classical massage can give a similar feeling – calm, refreshed, as if you've found back a part of yourself you didn't know was lost. Some feel energized, others just want a soft couch and a good book.
How should I prepare?
Come as you feel comfortable. We'll take care of the rest. The only thing we ask – give yourself permission to relax. Leave phones, worries and fast pace behind the door. This time is only for you.
