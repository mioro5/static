Contraindications

High-intensity magnetic therapy is not suitable in the following cases:

  • Cardiac pacemaker or defibrillator – magnetic field may disrupt device function
  • Pregnancy – not used during pregnancy for safety reasons
  • Metal implants in treatment area – except modern titanium implants
  • Acute infections – fever or acute inflammation in treatment area
  • Cancer – active cancer or suspicion of its presence
  • Children – to ensure safety of growing organism

Frequently Asked Questions

Is high-intensity magnetic therapy painful?
No! High-intensity magnetic therapy is completely painless. You will only feel gentle warmth in the treatment area. The device works with magnetic fields, not electricity, so there are no unpleasant sensations.
When will I feel the first improvement?
Many patients feel relief after the first session. Significant improvement usually occurs within 1-2 weeks. For fractures and serious injuries, complete recovery may take 4-6 weeks.
Are there any side effects?
High-intensity magnetic therapy is a very safe method. Very rarely, mild fatigue or dizziness may occur immediately after the session, which passes quickly. There are no long-term side effects.
How many sessions are usually needed?
It depends on the severity of the problem. For acute injuries, 3-6 sessions are often sufficient. Chronic problems usually require 8-15 sessions. We always create treatment plans individually.
Are the results permanent?
Yes! Since high-intensity magnetic therapy improves tissue condition at the cellular level, the results are lasting. Most patients feel improvement for months after the treatment course ends. For preventive maintenance, individual maintenance sessions may be recommended.
Is it suitable for elderly people?
Yes, high-intensity magnetic therapy is excellent for elderly people. Since it requires no medications or injections and has no side effects, it is safe even for people with chronic conditions. Many elderly patients have achieved excellent results.
