Bioenergetic Massage

Bioenergetic massage uses gentle electrical currents and bioelectric stimulation to increase skin elasticity and relieve muscle tension. Suitable for body and face treatments, improves circulation and reduces stress.

Modern biotechnology

Bioenergetic massage is an innovative therapy method that combines traditional massage with modern biotechnology. This method uses controlled microcurrents that mimic the body's natural electrical signals.

Unlike conventional massage, bioenergetic therapy adds a bioelectric stimulation component that deeply affects cellular-level processes and stimulates the body's natural healing mechanisms.

How does bioenergetic massage work?

Bioenergetic massage is based on the use of gentle electrical currents that:

Stimulates at cellular level

Microcurrents activate cell membrane permeability, improving cellular nutrient absorption and waste product elimination.

Increases collagen production

Bioelectric stimulation enhances fibroblast activity, which produces collagen and elastin - the foundations of skin elasticity.

Improves circulation

Microcurrents dilate blood vessels and increase blood supply, bringing more oxygen and nutrients to tissues.

Relaxes muscles

Controlled electrical impulses help release muscle tension and reduce the occurrence of cramps.

Reduces inflammation

Microcurrents help reduce tissue inflammation and accelerate recovery processes.

Activates nervous system

Stimulates endorphin release and activates the parasympathetic nervous system, reducing stress.

Important aftercare

After bioenergetic massage, it's important to follow recommendations for optimal results:

  • Drink plenty of water - at least 2 liters daily to support metabolism
  • Avoid intense physical activity on the first day
  • Use moisturizing cream in the treatment area to maintain skin tone
  • Rest adequately - give your body time to engage in healing processes
  • Avoid hot shower or sauna for 4-6 hours after treatment
  • Limit alcohol consumption on treatment day and the next day

Contraindications

Bioenergetic massage is not suitable for everyone. Please inform the therapist if you have:

  • Pacemaker or other implanted electronic devices
  • Pregnancy (any trimester)
  • Recent surgical wounds or injuries
  • Acute infections or fever
  • Blood disorders or thrombosis risk
  • Epilepsy
  • Metal implants in the treatment area
  • Skin sensitivity disorders

Frequently asked questions

Is bioenergetic massage safe?
Yes, bioenergetic massage is completely safe when performed by a qualified therapist. The microcurrents used are very low and mimic the body's natural electrical activity. The procedure is painless and relaxing.
What does bioenergetic massage feel like?
Most people describe bioenergetic massage as a gentle tingling or warmth. The sensation is usually pleasant and relaxing. Some may feel very gentle vibration, but it should not be painful or uncomfortable.
When will I see results?
Immediate relaxation and wellbeing are usually felt right away. Skin elasticity improvement and muscle tension reduction become visible after 3-5 sessions. Best results are achieved with a regular treatment course.
How often should bioenergetic massage be done?
Depending on goals, we recommend initially 1-2 times per week, then monthly for maintenance. For face treatments once a week, for body every 10-14 days. The therapist will create an individual treatment plan for you.
Can bioenergetic massage be combined with other services?
Yes, bioenergetic massage combines well with many other services such as classical massage, lymphatic drainage, or facial treatments. The therapist will recommend the best combination based on your needs.
