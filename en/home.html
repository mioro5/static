Frequently Asked Questions

What problems is medical massage intended for?
Medical massage helps with chronic muscle pain, nerve impingement, migraines, and joint mobility restrictions. It focuses on treating specific problems rather than general relaxation.
How does medical massage differ from regular massage?
Medical massage is targeted therapy using deep tissue techniques and anatomical knowledge to address specific health issues. Regular massage focuses on general relaxation.
How often should I get medical massage?
During active treatment, we recommend sessions at 4-7 day intervals. After achieving results, monthly maintenance sessions are sufficient. Your therapist will create a personalized plan.
Can medical massage be painful?
Medical massage is more intensive than regular massage but should never cause excessive pain. The therapist adjusts pressure based on your feedback ‚Äî open communication during sessions is essential.
How to prepare for massage and what to do after?
Avoid heavy food before the session and stay hydrated. After massage, drink 1-2 liters of water, do light movement like walking, and rest. Intensive training immediately after is not recommended.
What is magnetic therapy and is it safe?
Magnetic therapy is a non-invasive treatment using high-intensity magnetic fields. It helps with chronic pain and nerve issues. MV Therapy uses modern professional PEMF/EMTT equipment.
