Hands as Wings - Massage Training for Self-Treatment and Relaxation

Training program description: Hands as Wings training program is designed for those who want to learn various massage techniques to relieve everyday tension and stress. The program focuses on the hands, upper body, fingers, and neck areas, and teaches different techniques such as palpation, trigger point therapy, PIR technique, STR (Soft Tissue Release) techniques, and much more.

The aim of the training program is to help learners acquire the necessary skills and knowledge to care for and treat their own body. Learners will learn how to apply massage techniques to different areas of the body to reduce daily stress and tension.

Training program content:

Introduction to massage techniques and their use in different areas of the body
Palpation and muscle tension detection
Trigger point therapy and its use for pain relief
Using PIR technique to stretch and relax muscles
Learning STR techniques and their application
Use of massage oil and its impact on health
Practical exercises and application of techniques in different situations

Training duration and format: The training lasts a total of 16 hours and is divided into two days. The training is hands-on, where learners can immediately practice what they have learned and do exercises with other participants.

Training program results:

Ability to relieve daily tension and stress through massage
Ability to detect muscle tension and make conclusions about them
Knowledge of various massage techniques and their use in different body areas
Ability to use massage oil and know its impact on health
Acquisition of self-treatment methods and ability to apply them in daily life

Who is the training for:

People who want to learn self-massage techniques
People who want to reduce daily tension and stress
Sports enthusiasts who want to learn muscle stretching and relaxation
Anyone who wants to care for their body more healthily and acquire self-treatment methods.

Training prerequisites:

No prior massage experience is required
Anyone interested can register for the training
Desire to learn new self-treatment methods and care for their body.
