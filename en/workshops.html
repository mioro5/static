Professional Massage Training Courses in Pärnu and Estonia

Take your skills to the next level with our certified massage training courses. We offer evidence-based techniques both in Pärnu and across Estonia for various target groups – from beginners to experienced massage and physiotherapists.
Training schedules can be followed on this page or our Facebook page. If you wish to arrange training at your premises or company anywhere in Estonia, please write to us at info@mvtherapy.ee or call +372 58189561. Training takes place mainly in Pärnu, but we are ready to travel across Estonia.

Why choose MV Therapy training in Estonia?

Small groups (max 8 participants) for a personal approach
Evidence-based techniques and modern learning materials
Practical exercises and real case studies
Training certificate and follow-up support
Flexible schedule and corporate training options
