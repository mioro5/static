Frequently Asked Questions About Our Therapist

What qualifications do MV Therapy massage therapists have?

Our massage therapist holds a Level 6 professional certificate (#219003) issued by Kutsekoda. Additionally, they work as an instructor at the Estonian School of Massage and Therapy and are pursuing medical education at Tallinn Health Care College.

What is a Level 6 certificate in massage therapy?

A Level 6 certificate is the highest qualification level in massage therapy in Estonia. This means the therapist has comprehensive knowledge and practical skills for conducting professional massage treatments.

Is the MV Therapy therapist an instructor?

Yes, our massage therapist works as an instructor at the Estonian School of Massage and Therapy, sharing knowledge and experience in training new specialists.

Is the therapist a member of a professional association?

Yes, our massage therapist is an active member of the Estonian Massage Union, which ensures adherence to professional standards and continuous professional development.
