Booking Terms & Conditions

Booking Cancellation, Changes, and No-Show

Timely cancellation (more than 24 hours in advance): the paid amount is not refunded in cash but is credited to the client's account as a voucher or credit for future use.

Late cancellation (less than 24 hours in advance) or no-show: treated as a breach of contract. The client is charged a 100% compensation of the full booking amount.

For bookings made with prepayment or gift card: the prepayment or gift card is forfeited at 100%.

For cash payments: MV Therapy reserves the right to issue a damage compensation invoice.

New bookings are only possible after the compensation fee has been paid.

General Rules for Prepayments and Gift Cards

Non-refundable: prepayments and gift cards are not refundable in cash. Unused amounts remain as credit on the client's account.

Validity period: all prepayments and gift cards are valid for 3 months from the date of purchase.

Third-party gift cards (e.g., Stebby): subject to the intermediary's rules and conditions.

Client Late Arrival

Up to 15 minutes late: the session is conducted in a shortened format but charged at full price.

More than 15 minutes late: the session is cancelled and counted as a no-show. No-show rules apply.

Health Condition and Intoxication

Massage is not performed on clients who are under the influence of alcohol or drugs.

Refusal of service in this case is treated as a late cancellation, and the corresponding rules apply.

Repeated Rescheduling

A booking can be rescheduled a maximum of 2 times, provided at least 24 hours' notice is given.

On the third rescheduling attempt, the booking is cancelled and cancellation rules apply.

Refusal of Service

MV Therapy reserves the right to refuse service to clients who behave rudely, threateningly, or disruptively.

Unused credit remains valid for 3 months and can be transferred to another person.

Professional Boundaries

During the massage session, the client must maintain professional boundaries and behave appropriately.

If professional boundaries are violated, the session is terminated immediately.

All prepayments and gift cards are forfeited at 100% without compensation.

Periodic Invoicing for Companies

We issue a consolidated invoice for the previous month's services to corporate clients.

No-shows are invoiced at 100% of the session value.
