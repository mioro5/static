Usein kysytyt kysymykset

Mihin ongelmiin lääketieteellinen hieronta on tarkoitettu?
Lääketieteellinen hieronta helps with chronic muscle pain, nerve impingement, migraines, and joint mobility restrictions. It focuses on treating specific problems rather than general relaxation.
Miten lääketieteellinen hieronta eroaa tavallisesta hieronnasta?
Lääketieteellinen hieronta is targeted therapy using deep tissue techniques and anatomical knowledge to address specific health issues. Regular massage focuses on general relaxation.
Kuinka usein minun pitäisi käydä lääketieteellisessä hieronnassa?
During active treatment, we recommend sessions at 4-7 day intervals. After achieving results, monthly maintenance sessions are sufficient. Your therapist will create a personalized plan.
Voiko lääketieteellinen hieronta olla kivulias?
Lääketieteellinen hieronta is more intensive than regular massage but should never cause excessive pain. The therapist adjusts pressure based on your feedback — open communication during sessions is essential.
How to prepare for massage and what to do after?
Avoid heavy food before the session and stay hydrated. After massage, drink 1-2 liters of water, do light movement like walking, and rest. Intensive training immediately after is not recommended.
What is magnetic therapy and is it safe?
Magnetic therapy is a non-invasive treatment using high-intensity magnetic fields. It helps with chronic pain and nerve issues. MV Therapy uses modern professional PEMF/EMTT equipment.
